## Methodology

**On-chain investigations** follow a consistent workflow:

1. Identify seed address from public sources
2. Trace transactions via Arkham Intelligence, mempool.space, and Blockchair
3. Classify address types and detect clustering patterns
4. Identify timing anomalies, fee anomalies, and structural patterns
5. Map findings to ACAMS CAMS typology framework
6. Cross-reference with FATF VA Guidance and Basel AML Index
7. Apply jurisdictional analysis
8. Identify residual risk and VASP obligations

**Regulatory & framework analyses** follow a separate workflow:

1. Review primary sources (legislation, regulatory resolutions, official guidance)
2. Map obligations to affected entities (VASPs, banks, payment institutions)
3. Compare across jurisdictions (Brazil · US · EU · FATF standards)
4. Identify practical gaps between regulatory text and operational reality
5. Assess AML/CFT exposure and supervisory implications

**Tools used:** Arkham Intelligence · mempool.space · Blockchair · OXT · OSINT public sources
